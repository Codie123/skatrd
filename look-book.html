Skate Change Life.

Presenting you with the SKATRD transeasonal collection "SKATE CHANGE LIFE" which comes with a line of baggy, casual and hard wear clothing. This new skate wear collection of SKATRD consists of 5 looks which are comfy, stylish and comes with utility in every product.It is a representation of the culture which talks about being free, comfortable, unique and creative, Transeasonal skate pants, oversized shirts and shackets are done in a mix of technical and classic materials like cotton, denims, corduroy and flannel . AS for skate wear it comes with an amazing quality of being durable , so you can fall as many times as you want without thinking about the clothes getting torn off.
