CARVED 002.

We at SKATRD, are very excited to announce that we are launching our first CAPSULE COLLECTION named as CARVED 002. With our collection, we want to take you back to the old times where baggy, oversized, hip was a trendsetter. With keeping that in mind we carefully infused retro culture with the evolution of skateboards. A bit confusing right? Let me clear it for you guys.

Our designs are created in a way which will give you the knowledge of the evolution of skateboarding. As we all know skateboarding started with nothing but a wooden plank, after the years of gaining the interest of the people skateboards started evolving keeping the comfort and the style hand in hand. With different years came different designs of the skateboards making it more creative and more comfortable to use. Every design was created in such a way that it goes well with the sport making it easy to ride and do tricks.
