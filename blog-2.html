Go Skateboarding Day, 2023

Written by Yushika Malhotra  7th july 2023

21st June 2023, a special day for SKATRD, it wasn't just a regular summer day of June but an eventful day for everyone in and around Kannur, where all skaters gathered at Lighthouse Road and celebrated what is known as the Go Skateboarding Day. A total of 25-30 skaters from in and around Kannur, joined for Go Skateboarding Day. It was a day where our team interacted with new skaters and everyone got to showcase their skills. The day started with a general introduction about the sport and the culture which was taken forward to a cruising session from Lighthouse Road to Payyambalam Beach Road followed by a skate jam where every skater showcased their talent in skateboarding.

We had contests like highest ollies, longest run and of course the Game of Skates. Not just in Kannur, SKATRD team went to Goa for the Monsoon Skatejam at Cirrus, Anjuna where we joined the Skate Life Goa community and skateboarded with them at the newly opened skatepark at Cirrus. By the end of the event, we organised a giveaway, where people got the chance to get our USS 04.01 T shirts and other SKATRD products as a part of skateboarding contests prize. Indeed a happy day and fun day for SKATRD.
